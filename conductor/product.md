# Product Definition: AI CFO

## Vision
An AI-powered personal and small business financial officer designed for the modern era. It leverages the speed and global reach of Cloudflare's edge network to provide instant financial insights, automated tracking, and strategic advice without the overhead of traditional server-side environments.

## Target Audience
- **Freelancers/Solopreneurs:** Automating financial tracking to focus on their craft.
- **Personal Finance Users:** Simple yet powerful tools for managing budgets and expenses.
- **Small Business Owners:** Real-time visibility into cash flow and financial health.

## Core Features (MVP)
- **Automated Expense Categorization:** AI-driven classification of expenses from various inputs.
- **Financial Insights & Advice:** Proactive suggestions on saving, investing, and optimizing financial decisions.
- **Cash Flow Forecasting:** Predicting future financial states based on historical data and trends.
- **Data-Rich Dashboard:** A visual-first interface for at-a-glance financial analysis.

## Strategic Goals
- **Edge-First Performance:** Utilizing Cloudflare Workers and Hono for sub-millisecond responses.
- **Zero-NodeJS Dependency:** Optimized for the Cloudflare runtime to ensure maximum compatibility and speed.
- **Integrated AI:** Deep integration with Google Gemini for sophisticated financial analysis.
- **Secure Authentication:** Robust user identity management via BetterAuth.
- **Durable Storage:** Using Cloudflare D1 for a resilient, edge-optimized SQL database.
